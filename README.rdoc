== Prescription Medication Database

Database of Florida patients and and their full prescription history. Case Project for Damien Alpizar and Team for doctoral pharmacy case competition. 

== Patient Information

`Patient Name, Date of Birth, Address and Insurance information`

== Patient History

Each patient can have a history of prescriptions, ranging from provider, pharmacy and fill date.

`Prescription, Quantity, Fill Date, Provider, NPI, Pharmacy ID, Phone`
